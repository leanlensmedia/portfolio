Long-form YouTube editing and short-form clips built for retention. Every cut has a reason. Every video is optimized before it goes live.

Common questions

Before you reach out

Do I need to already have a YouTube channel?

No. We have built channels from under 100 subscribers. What matters is that you are committed to showing up consistently. We handle everything else.

⌄Click to learn more
We have worked with creators at every stage. Whether you have zero subscribers or 600K, the system is the same. Strong hooks, consistent output, and a packaging strategy built around your specific audience. The channel size at the start does not determine where you end up.
What do you actually need from me?

Your raw footage and a general direction of what you want to say. We write the hook, structure the edit, and handle everything from there.

⌄Click to learn more
Beyond the footage, it helps to have a rough idea of the topic. Even just a sentence. From there we handle the scripting direction, hook writing, pacing decisions, captions, sound design, thumbnail brief, and the blueprint for the next video. Most clients spend about 30 minutes a month on the planning side. Everything else is handled.
How long before I see results?

The first month is about building the system. Most creators see traction between month two and three. Consistent growth typically shows between months three and five.

⌄Click to learn more
Month one is foundation. We establish the format, the hook style, and the production rhythm. Month two and three the data starts talking. By month four and five you have enough output to see clear patterns in what your audience responds to. We track everything so you always know where things stand and why.
Do you work with creators outside of fitness?

Right now no. Fitness, movement, and calisthenics is where we go deep. That focus is intentional. It is what makes the work better.

⌄Click to learn more
We understand the fitness audience, the terminology, the programming, and what makes people watch and trust a creator in this space. That context is not something you can fake. It is built over years of training and working exclusively in this niche. Going deep in one area beats being average everywhere.
What about VSLs and paid ads?

Yes. If you have an audience and you are ready to monetize it with a sales video or paid campaign, we handle that separately from monthly packages.

⌄Click to learn more
VSLs and paid ad creative are project-based, not retainer-based. We scope each one based on the length, format, and what it needs to convert. Starting from $500 for short-form ads. If you have a program, a course, or a product and you want a video that sells it, reach out and we will talk through what makes sense for your situation.
